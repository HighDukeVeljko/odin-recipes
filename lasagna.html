Lasagna

This is a simple lasagna recipe.

Home

This crockpot lasagna recipe is so easy, you might think that you missed something. It is a delicious slow cooker meal!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 (29 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 16 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 12 ounces cottage cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 (12 ounce) package lasagna noodles

Directions:

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook and stir ground beef, onion, and garlic until meat is brown and crumbly. Drain off fat. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, salt, and oregano. Simmer for about 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. In a bowl, mix together mozzarella cheese, cottage cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
  3. Spread 1 cup of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 5- or 6-quart slow cooker. Layer with 3 noodles, half of the cheese mixture, and 1 cup of the meat sauce. Repeat layers once more. Top with remaining noodles and meat sauce.
  4. Cover and cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours or on High for 3 to 4 hours.
